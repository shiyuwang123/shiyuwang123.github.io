Most people deem them as an intact entity, whose parts work harmoniously towards common goals. However, anyone who learnt high school level biology knows that inside our body, conflicts between different substances, different cells, different organs are ubiquitous. Moreover, there are numerous conflicts inside the huge neural network inside our brain. These conflicts actually create our internal politics, which greatly influence our decisions and behaviors. When we didn't realize the existence of internal politics, it just happens spontaneously. As we cognize it, we can deliberately manipulate it to achieve our goals.
